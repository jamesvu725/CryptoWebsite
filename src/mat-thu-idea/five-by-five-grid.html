5x5 Grid

Substitute each letter in the message using the numbers it corresponds to in a 5x5 grid. You read the coordinates by row then column. Y and Z shares the same square.

Example:

Grid = 22 43 24 14

Substitute each letter in your message with the corresponding number using the table below.

Example:

Original Message: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Encrypted Message: 45 23 15 / 42 51 24 13 31 / 12 43 35 53 34 / 21 35 54 / 25 51 33 41 44 / 35 52 15 43 / 45 23 15 / 32 11 55 55 / 14 35 22

Substitute each number in your message with the corresponding letter using the table below.

Example:

Encrypted Message: 45 23 15 / 42 51 24 13 31 / 12 43 35 53 34 / 21 35 54 / 25 51 33 41 44 / 35 52 15 43 / 45 23 15 / 32 11 55 55 / 14 35 22

Decrypted Message: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
